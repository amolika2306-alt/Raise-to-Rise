Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: This website does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics. Payments go directly to a personal UPI address — no financial data passes through this website.

1. Who we are

Raise to Rise Foundation is a grassroots community initiative based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, founded by Amolika Reddy. We are not a registered NGO, trust, or society. All contributions are voluntary personal donations.

Contact: raisetorisecom@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We collect no personal data through this website. Specifically:

  • We do not run any forms that capture your name, email, or contact details
  • We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other tracking tool
  • We do not log visitor IP addresses
  • We do not use advertising networks

3. Cookies

This website does not set any tracking or analytics cookies.

Our hosting provider (Netlify) may set essential technical cookies required to serve the website. These are functional only and contain no personal information. You can review Netlify's data practices at netlify.com/privacy.

4. Payments

Donations are made directly via UPI (Unified Payments Interface) using the QR code or UPI ID displayed on this website. This means:

  • All payment processing happens entirely within your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • No financial or card data is entered on or transmitted through this website
  • This website has no access to your payment information
  • Donations are voluntary personal transfers and are not tax-deductible

5. Emails

If you contact us via email, we will use your email address solely to respond to your message. We do not subscribe you to any mailing list or share your address with anyone.

6. Third-party services

This website uses one external service:

  • QR Code API (api.qrserver.com) — used to generate the UPI QR code image. Your browser makes a request to this service to load the QR image. No personal data is sent; only the UPI payment string is included in the image request.

7. External links

Clicking email or UPI links will open your email or payment app respectively. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those applications.

8. Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Accessibility

We are committed to making this website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. This site is built to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines:

  • All images have descriptive alt text
  • The site is fully navigable by keyboard alone, with a visible skip-to-content link
  • Video dialogs trap focus and return it correctly when closed
  • Decorative elements are hidden from screen readers with aria-hidden
  • Motion is paused automatically if your device has reduced-motion enabled
  • Semantic landmarks (nav, main, footer) help screen readers navigate the page

If you encounter any accessibility barrier, email raisetorisecom@gmail.com and we will address it promptly.

10. Security

This is a static site with no server-side processing, no user accounts, and no database. Security measures in place:

  • HTTPS enforced on all connections (HSTS, 1-year max-age, preload)
  • Content Security Policy blocking unauthorized scripts, frames, and outbound connections
  • X-Frame-Options set to DENY, preventing clickjacking
  • Permissions Policy disabling camera, microphone, geolocation, and other browser APIs
  • Common attack paths (WordPress admin, PHP files, .env, .git) blocked at the CDN edge
  • No third-party trackers, analytics scripts, or ad networks

11. Changes to this policy

If we make any material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy, security, or accessibility? Email raisetorisecom@gmail.com.
