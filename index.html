Our mission

We started small.
On purpose.

Raise to Rise is a grassroots initiative built on one belief: when we lift others, we all rise. We began with one person and a few drives, doing real work on the ground in Hyderabad's underserved communities.

No salaries. No office costs. Founded by Amolika Reddy, we run pad distribution drives, food drives, and sanitation awareness sessions. We show you exactly where every rupee goes.

What we do

Three drives.
One neighbourhood at a time.

Pad distribution

Sanitary pads for girls and women in underserved communities, because period poverty quietly keeps girls out of school.

Food drives

Warm meals and essential supplies distributed across communities in Hyderabad, with help from local volunteers.

Sanitation education

Menstrual health and hygiene sessions in government schools, pairing supplies with knowledge that lasts.
