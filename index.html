Our mission

We started small. On purpose.

Raise to Rise is a Hyderabad-based grassroots initiative built on one belief: when we lift others, we all rise. We began with one person and a few drives — real work on the ground. Now we're growing into a community of volunteers and everyday givers who prove that small, honest amounts can do real good.

What we do

Three drives, one neighbourhood at a time.

🌸

Pad distribution

Sanitary pads for girls and women in underserved communities — because period poverty quietly keeps girls out of school.

🍲

Food drives

Warm meals and essential food packs organised across slum communities in Hyderabad, with help from local volunteers.

🧼

Sanitation mentoring

Hygiene and sanitation sessions in government schools — pairing supplies with the knowledge to use them well.
